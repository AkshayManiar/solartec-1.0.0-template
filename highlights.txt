Currently Working on 230 km, 230 kV Transmission Line with Lattice Guyed Structures

July 16, 2014

Chimax is currently working on the detailed design of a 230kV transmission line that spans 230 km long in Eastern Canada. Free standing lattice steel towers are used for the dead end & heavy angle structures with steel guyed-V structures being used for the tangent and light angle structures. Rock anchoring and grillage foundations are utilized in this project.

Chimax Awarded Highway 401 and 407 Transmission Line Crossing Project

July 16, 2014

Chimax is working on the detailed design for two sets of power lines where they will cross the HWY 401 and HWY 407 interchange. Two sets of mono steel poles structures will be used, one on either side of HWY 407, with one set of lines being used by a local telecom and the other set by a public utility. The project is slated for completion in 2015 near Ajax and Whitby.
