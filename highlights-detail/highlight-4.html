Chimax Plays a Major Role in Creating Cogeneration and Renewable energy Infrastructure in Ontario

Chimax has vast experience in engineering co-generation high voltage substations and has successfully completed many cogeneration projects.

Notable projects include:

  • Greenfield cogeneration facility (Ontario)
  • Halton Hill cogeneration facility (Ontario)
  • Spy Hill cogeneration facility (Saskatchewan)
  • East Windsor cogeneration facility (Ontario)
  • Thorold cogeneration facility (Ontario)
  • York Region cogeneration facility (Ontario)
  • St. Clair cogeneration facility (Ontario)
  • Transalta Sarnia cogeneration facility (Ontario)

In addition, Chimax has played a major role in the renewable energy infrastructure in Ontario. Chimax has completed many solar power, wind power, and hydro electrical projects, which include:
