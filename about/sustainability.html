Sustainability

Responsible Engineering for a Changing Future

At Chimax Inc., sustainability is integrated into how we plan, design, and operate. Our approach focuses on delivering long-term value while supporting environmental responsibility, social accountability, and resilient infrastructure development.

We are committed to aligning our operations with recognized corporate responsibility principles, ensuring that health, safety, environmental stewardship, and ethical business practices remain central to everything we do.

Our Approach to Sustainability

Environmental Responsibility

Chimax actively works to reduce its environmental footprint by promoting efficient use of resources and energy within our operations. Our engineering solutions are developed with consideration for environmental impact, long-term asset performance, and evolving climate conditions.

Climate Awareness & Resilience

We recognize the impact of climate change on infrastructure. Chimax has evaluated climate-related risks to its operations and incorporates mitigation strategies into planning and design. Our Business Continuity Plan (BCP) ensures operational resilience and preparedness in the event of disruptions.

Governance & Ethical Practices

We maintain formal policies that support transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct, including procedures for reporting unethical behavior. Our governance framework aligns with recognized standards such as ISO 26000, supporting responsible business operations across all levels of the organization.

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Chimax is committed to fostering an inclusive workplace. Our Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) policies promote fair and respectful engagement across employees, partners, and stakeholders.

Health & Safety Integration

Sustainability at Chimax begins with people. Our health and safety policies extend across employees, suppliers, and stakeholders, ensuring safe and responsible practices throughout all phases of our work.

Policies & Standards

Chimax has established and maintains a range of policies and procedures that support its sustainability commitments:

Health & Safety Management
Reporting Unethical Behavior
Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)
Sustainability Reporting Framework
Social Responsibility (ISO 26000)
Environmental Management System
Climate Change Risk Framework
Business Continuity Plan (BCP)
Energy & Resource Reduction
GHG Measurement & Monitoring

Accountability & Continuous Improvement

Chimax is committed to ongoing improvement through regular monitoring, reporting, and evaluation of its sustainability performance. Our practices align with recognized frameworks, and we continue to strengthen our approach to environmental and social responsibility.

Annual Corporate report available upon request.
