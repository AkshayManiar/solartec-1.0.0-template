Project Management

Chimax offers professional project management services for utility, industrial, and renewable energy projects across Canada. With decades of experience in transmission, distribution, and substation projects, our team ensures that engineering, construction, and commissioning activities are coordinated efficiently and delivered on time and within budget.

Our Services Include

  • Project planning and scheduling
  • Budgeting, cost control, and risk management
  • Procurement and contractor coordination
  • Construction management and quality assurance
  • Permitting and regulatory compliance oversight
  • Stakeholder communication and reporting
  • Commissioning and close-out support
Reliable Execution

By combining technical expertise with strong project management practices, Chimax ensures projects are executed safely, efficiently, and to the highest standards, from concept through to successful completion.
