Our Services

Specialized Engineering Solutions

Chimax Inc. provides specialized civil and structural engineering services for the power and energy sector. Our expertise includes the design and engineering of high-voltage substations, switching stations, and transmission and distribution networks.

With extensive experience supporting Ontario’s renewable energy expansion, Chimax has contributed to a significant portion of the province’s wind and solar power developments. This background allows us to deliver practical, utility-aligned engineering solutions from concept through construction support, with a strong emphasis on safety, reliability, and value.
