Health and Safety

A Culture Built on Safety

A Safe Workplace Is a Shared Commitment

At Chimax Inc., safety is a fundamental responsibility embedded in everything we do. It is not treated as an afterthought, but as a core part of our daily operations—both in office environments and on project sites. We are committed to proactively identifying risks, controlling hazards, and ensuring that safe practices are consistently followed.

Our objective is clear: every individual goes home safely at the end of each day.

With over 35 years of experience, Chimax has developed a safety culture built on accountability, awareness, and continuous improvement. We operate in full compliance with the Ontario Occupational Health and Safety Act, supported by structured internal processes that promote transparency and responsibility at every level.

Employee Involvement & Responsibility

Our safety culture is driven by active employee participation. We encourage our team members to take ownership of safety—not only for themselves but for their colleagues. Through regular training, including CPR and First Aid certification, and ongoing communication, employees are equipped to recognize hazards, make informed decisions, and contribute to a safer workplace.

Our Joint Health and Safety Committee (JHSC) plays a key role in this process, conducting regular inspections and providing a platform for continuous feedback and improvement. This collaborative approach strengthens engagement, reinforces accountability, and helps maintain a safe and respectful working environment.

Our Journey

Building Practical Engineering Solutions Since 1994

Chimax Inc. was established in 1994 with a clear focus on delivering reliable and practical engineering solutions for the power utility industry. Founded under the leadership of experienced engineers, the company began by supporting utilities and industrial clients with specialized engineering design services.

Over the years, Chimax has steadily expanded its capabilities and expertise, growing into a trusted engineering partner for transmission and distribution infrastructure projects. With a strong foundation in technical excellence and a commitment to quality, the company has successfully delivered more than 500 projects across Canada and beyond.

Our journey has been defined by continuous learning, adaptation, and a focus on real-world project needs. From early work in substations and industrial structures to complex transmission line and renewable energy projects, Chimax has consistently evolved to meet the changing demands of the industry.

Today, Chimax supports utilities, contractors, and power developers with comprehensive engineering services that prioritize constructability, efficiency, and long-term performance. While the scale and complexity of our projects have grown, our core approach remains the same—delivering practical, reliable solutions that meet client expectations.

As we look ahead, Chimax continues to build on its experience, strengthening its capabilities and contributing to the development of resilient and sustainable power infrastructure.
