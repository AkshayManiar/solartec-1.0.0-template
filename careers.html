Why Join Us!

Build Your Career in Power Infrastructure Engineering

At Chimax, we design the infrastructure that keeps power systems operating safely and reliably. Our success is driven by skilled professionals who value technical excellence, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

We are always interested in connecting with engineers, designers, and technical professionals who are passionate about civil and structural engineering in the power sector.

Experienced Professionals

Chimax offers opportunities for engineers and designers who are looking to work on complex transmission, substation, and renewable energy projects. When you join Chimax, you can expect:

  • Exposure to high-voltage infrastructure projects
  • A collaborative, technically focused environment
  • Opportunities to work with industry-standard tools
  • Professional growth through challenging assignments
  • A flexible and supportive workplace culture
We value initiative, technical discipline, and a commitment to quality engineering.

Students & New Graduates

We welcome students and recent graduates who are eager to develop their careers in power infrastructure engineering. Our internship and co-op opportunities provide:

  • Hands-on experience in real engineering projects
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Exposure to utility standards and industry practices
  • Practical learning beyond academic coursework
Chimax provides a strong foundation for building technical confidence and industry knowledge.
