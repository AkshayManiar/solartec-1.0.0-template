Frequently Asked Questions
1. What engineering services does Chimax provide?
Chimax specializes in civil and structural engineering for high-voltage substations, switchyards, and transmission & distribution lines.
2. What voltage levels do you support?
We design infrastructure ranging from 13.8 kV distribution systems up to 500 kV transmission and substation facilities.
3. Do you provide complete design packages for construction?
Yes. We deliver construction-ready engineering packages including drawings, calculations, bills of materials, and specifications.
4. Does Chimax perform route selection and clearance analysis for transmission lines?
Yes. We provide route optimization, structure spotting, sag and tension analysis, and clearance verification.
5. Do you design substation layouts and foundations?
Yes. Our services include equipment layout, structural steel design, foundation engineering, grading plans, and lightning protection coordination.
6. What engineering standards do you follow?
Our designs comply with CSA, IEEE, ASCE, and client- or utility-specific standards and regulations.
7. Do you support renewable energy projects?
Yes. We provide engineering services for wind, solar, BESS, hydropower, and grid interconnection projects.
8. Can Chimax assist during construction?
Yes. We offer technical support, design clarification, field revisions, and construction-phase engineering assistance.
9. Do you provide feasibility studies and cost estimation?
Yes. We support early-stage planning with feasibility assessments and preliminary cost evaluations.
10. Does Chimax offer owner’s engineering or third-party review services?
Yes. We provide independent design reviews, technical advisory services, and owner’s engineering support.
